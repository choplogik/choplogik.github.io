Choplogik is Russell Alton, organically produced in Smithers, BC.

Russell Alton builds an imaginative universe filled with friendship, joy & magic and has it leak into & hopefully influence our own. They enjoy taking the familiar & setting it slightly askew, so that the viewer will consider it anew. They scribble, tattoo, & meet cats on the street in Vancouver. They have exhibited in BC & the US and their work is collected worldwide.

If you write 'em a nice letter addressed to choplogik [at] gmail.com, they'll write you one back :)
