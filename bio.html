Chop-Logik is Russell Alton, organically produced in Smithers, BC.

Russell Alton builds an imaginative universe filled with friendship, joy & magic and has it leak into & hopefully influence our own. He enjoys taking the familiar & setting it slightly askew, so that the viewer will consider it anew. He now scribbles, paints & prints butts in Vancouver. He has exhibited in BC & the US and his work is collected worldwide.

If you write him a nice letter addressed to choplogik [at] gmail.com, he'll write you one back.
